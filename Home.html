Deforestation:
Large-scale clearing of forests for agriculture, urbanization, and industrializationhas led to habitat destruction and loss of biodiversity.
Pollution:
Industrial and agricultural pollution contaminates soil, water sources, and air, negatively impacting both wildlife and human health.
Desertification:
Overgrazing, poor agricultural practices, and deforestation contribute to soil degradation, turning fertile lands into deserts.
Illegal Wildlife Trade:
Poaching and trafficking of endangered species threaten biodiversity and disrupt ecosystems.
